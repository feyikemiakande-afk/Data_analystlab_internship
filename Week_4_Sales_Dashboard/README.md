# Week 4: Superstore Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview

This project was completed as part of **Week 4 of the AnalystLab Africa Data Analytics Internship**, focusing on **Data Visualization and Dashboarding** using Microsoft Power BI.

The objective was to transform raw retail sales data into an interactive business intelligence dashboard that helps stakeholders monitor performance, identify trends, and make informed business decisions. The dashboard explores sales, profitability, customer behaviour, product performance, and regional performance across the Superstore dataset.

---

## Business Problem

The Superstore dataset contains several years of sales data, but without effective analysis, it is difficult to understand what drives business performance.

This dashboard was built to answer key business questions such as:

- Which regions generate the highest sales and profit?
- Which products perform well and which reduce profitability?
- How has sales performance changed over time?
- How do discounts affect profit?
- Which customer segments contribute the most value?

---

## Dataset

The dashboard uses the **Superstore Sales Dataset**, a fictional retail dataset containing **9,994 records** across **21 columns**, covering sales transactions from **2016 to 2019**.

The dataset includes:

- Order information
- Customer details
- Product categories and sub-categories
- Geographic information
- Sales, Quantity, Discount and Profit

---

## Dashboard Features

The dashboard consists of two interactive report pages.

### Page 1 – Sales Overview

- KPI Cards
- Sales Trend Analysis
- Profit by Region
- Geographic Sales Map
- Interactive slicers for Year, Category and Customer Segment

### Page 2 – Profit & Product Performance

- Product Performance Analysis
- Worst Performing Products
- Sales by Customer Segment
- Discount vs Profit Analysis
- Sales by Ship Mode
- Category Filters

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard tracks the following KPIs:

- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Total Orders
- Profit Margin (%)
- Average Order Value
- Year-over-Year Sales Growth
- Top Performing Region
- Best Selling Category

---

## Key Insights

The analysis revealed several important business insights:

- The **West Region** generated both the highest sales revenue and overall profit.
- **Tables** and **Bookcases** consistently produced negative profit despite generating healthy sales.
- Higher discounts were associated with lower profitability, particularly when discounts exceeded 20%.
- Although the **Consumer** segment placed the highest number of orders, **Corporate** customers recorded higher average order values.

---

## Recommendations

Based on the findings, the following recommendations were made:

- Reduce excessive discounts on underperforming furniture products.
- Increase investment in high-performing regions, particularly the West.
- Develop targeted sales strategies for Corporate customers.
- Review pricing and profitability of low-performing product categories.
